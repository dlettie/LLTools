Calculation Logic:
• 30-Day Factor: Appears from Day 30 onwards. = Sum(Treated last 30 days) / Sum(Control last 30 days)
• 60-Day Factor: Appears from Day 60 onwards. = Sum(Treated last 60 days) / Sum(Control last 60 days)
• 90-Day Factor: Appears from Day 90 onwards. = Sum(Treated last 90 days) / Sum(Control last 90 days)
